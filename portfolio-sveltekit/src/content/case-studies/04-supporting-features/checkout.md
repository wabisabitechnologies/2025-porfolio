# Checkout & Payments: Service Activation & Transactions

## Overview

SFL provides services:
- Company registration package
- Minute book + register templates
- Beneficial owner verification
- Annual compliance support

Companies need:
- Clear pricing
- Easy payment process
- Order tracking
- Invoice management
- Subscription renewals

---

## How SFL Handles It

**1. Service Packages**
- Choose package: Startup, Growth, Enterprise
- See what's included: Features, support level, price
- Transparent pricing: No hidden charges

**2. Checkout Process**
- Simple checkout: Add service, enter payment details
- Payment methods: Card, mobile money, bank transfer (Kenya-specific)
- Confirmation: Order confirmed, service activated immediately

**3. Invoice & Billing**
- Invoice generation: Automatic for accounting
- Billing cycle: Monthly, quarterly, or annual
- Renewal reminders: Notify before subscription expires

**4. Subscription Management**
- View active services: What's currently active?
- Upgrade/downgrade: Change package anytime
- Usage tracking: See how many companies, users, transactions

---

## Value

**For Clients:**
- Clear pricing (no surprises)
- Easy payment (multiple options)
- Transparent billing (know exactly what you're paying for)

**For SFL:**
- Automated revenue processing
- Recurring revenue (subscriptions)
- Usage-based metrics (scaling indicators)

## Client Story

**Scenario:** Company wants to activate SFL for their business

Step 1: Browse packages → "Growth package includes 10 companies, unlimited users, BO verification"
Step 2: Click "Subscribe" → Enter payment details (M-Pesa, card, or bank transfer)
Step 3: Payment confirmed → Company activated immediately
Step 4: System ready to use → Start setting up first company

**Outcome:**
- Fast activation (no waiting for approval)
- Clear understanding of what's included
- Transparent costs
