# SFL Admin View: Compliance & Reporting Dashboard

## Overview

SFL admin team needs:
- Real-time visibility into platform status
- Compliance metrics across all companies
- Practitioner performance tracking
- Regulatory risk alerts
- System health monitoring

---

## Key Features

**1. Platform Dashboard**
- Total companies on platform
- Total beneficial owners verified
- Compliance score (% of companies meeting all requirements)
- Risk alerts (companies at regulatory risk)

**2. Compliance Metrics**
- BO verification rate (% companies with all owners verified)
- Annual compliance completion (% companies current on filings)
- Article compliance (% decisions following governance rules)
- Audit readiness (% companies with complete audit trails)

**3. Practitioner Performance**
- By practitioner: Companies managed, compliance rate, client satisfaction
- Benchmarking: How does Practitioner A compare to average?
- Intervention triggers: If compliance drops, escalate support

**4. Regulatory Compliance**
- By regulator requirement (CBK, CMA, Companies Act)
- Non-compliance alerts
- Audit readiness reporting
- Regulatory inquiry response tracking

**5. System Health**
- Data integrity checks
- Audit trail completeness
- Performance monitoring
- Security event tracking

---

## Value

**For SFL:**
- Platform visibility
- Quality assurance
- Risk management
- Growth metrics (companies, practitioners, compliance)

**For Regulators:**
- If CBK audits SFL: "Here's our compliance oversight"
- Proof of systematic monitoring
- Confidence in platform stability

---

## Impact

When CBK/CMA audits SFL platform, admin view demonstrates:
- "We monitor all companies systematically"
- "Compliance is not optional, it's enforced"
- "Every governance action has audit trail"
- "We meet/exceed regulatory expectations"
