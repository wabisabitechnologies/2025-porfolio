"I've spent 5 years building SFL, a platform that makes Kenya's corporate governance professional, compliant, and scalable.

**The Problem:** Kenya's practices, company secretaries, and founders are drowning in manual governance administration. Minute books are scattered. Share registers live in Excel. Beneficial ownership verification is chaotic. Decision-making is slow. Compliance is an annual scramble.

**The Solution:** SFL makes governance operational, not ceremonial. Role-based permissions define authority. Business actions automatically generate statutory records. **The Proof:** 20 case studies showing specific workflows, time savings, risk reduction, and client transformations. Written with deep understanding of Kenya's regulatory landscape (CBK, Companies Act, CMA). Focused on what practitioners and clients actually need.
